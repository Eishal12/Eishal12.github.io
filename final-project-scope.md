# final project scope statement- Photography portfolio

- **Purpose:**
- create a clean, minimal  photography portfolio that showcase my work.
- Highlight four categories: nature, city scenes, night shots, and everyday moments.
- provide a simple online space where viewers can explore my photos easily. 

- **Main features:**
- A welcoming homepage with a short instructons.
- A gallery section divided into the four photography categories. 
- Simple navigation across all the pages.
- An email link for anyone who wants to reach out or give feedback. 

- **Audience:**
- Anyone interested in photography, including general viewers, friends,and family. 

- **Out of scope:**
- No e-commerce features. 
- No blogs or long-form writing sections. 
- No user accounts or login system.
- No heavy animations or complex interactive elements. 

- **Project summary:**
- This scope statement defines the goals, boundaries, and structure of the final project.
